Dat4T – The pre-processor for EngMod4T

 

Dat4T was developed by

 

Neels van Niekerk

of

Vannik Racing Developments

 

 

Dat4T is the preprocessor for the 1-dimensional gasdynamic multi cylinder four stroke engine simulation software, EngMod4T. This Help-system describes the required input variables and options available. It also describes the pipe numbering system and gives a brief overview of some of the models used.

 

The simulation models and simulation methods used in EngMod4T are mostly based on the work of Professor Emeritus Gordon P Blair of The Queen's University of Belfast.
